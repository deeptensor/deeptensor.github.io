The work plan for the next three years


  1. Investigation of possibilities and prospects of using various tensor networks for low-rank (compressed) data representation and development of new algorithms for tensor decompositions including tensor train and quantized tensor train decomposition.

    [ 2017. Executive in charge : I. Oseledets ]

  2. Investigation of optimization methods for functionals in the low-rank tensor format on tensor manifolds for effective low rank representation of tensor networks.

    [ 2017. Executive in charge : I. Oseledets ]

  3. Development of a new method for learning deep Bayesian generative models using generalized K samples importance lower bound to marginal likelihood.

    [ 2017. Executive in charge : D. Vetrov ]

  4. Development of a new second-order stochastic optimization method applicable to non-convex setting.

    [ 2017. Executive in charge : D. Vetrov ]

  5. Formation of a new database of images for experiments with deep neural networks.

    [ 2017. Executive in charge : V. Lempitsky ]

  6. Synthesis of deep learning and classical methods of computer vision (including cascade based detection, Hough transform, and the use of integral images) for fast image filtering.

    [ 2017. Executive in charge : V. Lempitsky ]
