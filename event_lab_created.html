Order on creation of the Laboratory is signed

November 1, 2016

President of the Skolkovo Institute of Science and Technology Alexander Kuleshov signed an order on creation of the Laboratory "Tensor networks and deep learning for applications in data mining" under the guidance of prof. Andrzej Cichocki. The Laboratory begins to operate from January, 2017.
