Лаборатория начинает набор исследователей, преподавателей и аспирантов

We opened several research positions for various levels including assistant professors, postdoc and PhD students. The Laboratory offers a unique research program and also PhD fellowship program, where PhD students can be co-supervised by two or even 3 advisors. Candidates are expected to take advantage of the opportunities offered by both very innovative research and to actively seek cross-group collaborations and in friendly and collaborative environment. The Skoltech also offers a wide range of activities like seminars, workshops, and summer schools, as well as the possibility to engage in organizing such events.
We encourage applications from outstanding candidates with motivation and passion with academic backgrounds in Applied Mathematics, Physics, Computer Science, Scientific Computing, Engineering, and related fields, and a keen interest in doing basic research in areas like: Machine Learning, Neural Networks, Complex Systems, Machine Intelligence, including Machine Vision and Natural Language Understanding, (Bio-Inspired) Robotic Systems, Data-Driven Computational Analysis, Big Data Analytic and Artificial Intelligence.
We are looking forward to receiving your online application consisting of a complete CV (incl. a list of publications, talks, projects and awards, if any), experience in programming, a short mission statement (1-2 pages) outlining the research interests of the candidate, and scanned transcripts of certificates (bachelor’s degree, master’s , PhD degree, other degrees).
Good knowledge of Matlab and/or Python is required.
For further information please contact: Prof. Ivan Oseledets, Prof. Victor Lempitsky and/or Prof. Andrzej Cichocki.
Apply now and take a unique opportunity!

15 Января 2017 г.
