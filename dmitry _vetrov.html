Scientific interests

Specialist in applied mathematics and computer science, including Bayesian regularization of machine learning algorithms, structured output learning, probabilistic graphical models, computer vision. Participated in several research projects on the development of new machine learning and probabilistic inference methods and in interdisciplinary research (cognitive sciences, medical data processing, non-organic chemistry, etc).
